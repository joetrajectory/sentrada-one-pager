- Qualisflow (Qflow): construction data capture and verification platform.
- What it sells: automated capture and verification of site delivery and waste
  data, creating an as-built truth layer for construction projects.
- Proof points: deployed with Tier-1 contractors including Bouygues on complex
  projects to improve financial performance; industry studies the company
  publishes show 5-11% of a project's materials budget is typically lost to
  waste, rework and unverified deliveries.
